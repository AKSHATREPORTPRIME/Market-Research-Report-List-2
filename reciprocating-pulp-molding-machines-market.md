<p><p>Reciprocating pulp molding machines are classified into two types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic machines are fully automated and require minimal human intervention. They have advanced features like automatic pulp feeding, forming, drying, and stacking. These machines are suitable for large-scale production and offer high efficiency. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual operation. Operators need to manually load the pulp, form the products, and transfer them for drying and stacking. These machines are suitable for smaller-scale production and provide flexibility and control to operators.</p></p>
