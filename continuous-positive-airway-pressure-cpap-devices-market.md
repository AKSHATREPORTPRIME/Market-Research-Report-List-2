The increasing prevalence of sleep apnea, technological advancements in device designs, and growing awareness regarding the benefits of CPAP therapy are major factors driving market growth. <p><p>Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) devices are used to treat sleep apnea. The fixed pressure CPAP device delivers a consistent level of air pressure throughout the night, helping to keep the airway open. On the other hand, auto-adjusting CPAP devices continuously monitor the patient's breathing patterns and adjust the air pressure accordingly. This type of device can provide varying levels of pressure as needed, ensuring optimal therapy throughout the night. Both types of CPAP devices aim to alleviate symptoms of sleep apnea and improve the quality of sleep for patients.</p></p>
