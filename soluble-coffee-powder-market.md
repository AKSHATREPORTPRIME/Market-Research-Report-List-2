<p><strong>The Soluble Coffee Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray-drying</li><li>Freeze-drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Soluble coffee powder refers to the instant coffee that is made by extracting the coffee flavor from coffee beans and drying it into a powder form. There are two main types of processes used to produce soluble coffee powder: spray-drying and freeze-drying. Spray-drying involves spraying a concentrated coffee solution into hot air, which evaporates the liquid and leaves behind the coffee powder. Freeze-drying, on the other hand, involves freezing the concentrated coffee solution and then removing the ice through sublimation, resulting in dry coffee powder. Both methods are used in the market to produce different types of soluble coffee powder.</p></p>
