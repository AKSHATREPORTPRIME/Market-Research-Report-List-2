<p><p>Ultra High Purity (UHP) Heat Exchangers are used in various industries to transfer heat between two fluids without mixing them. Shell & Tube Type UHP Heat Exchangers consist of a shell with multiple tubes inside, allowing for efficient heat transfer. Immersion Type UHP Heat Exchangers are submerged directly into the fluids, offering compact design and high heat transfer rates. Both types are specially designed for applications that require strict cleanliness, such as semiconductor manufacturing and pharmaceutical industries, ensuring the purity and quality of the fluids being processed.</p></p>
