<p><ul><li>Low Vacuum</li><li>Medium Vacuum</li><li>High Vacuum</li><li>Ultra-High Vacuum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum blower market can be categorized into four types based on the level of vacuum they can generate: low vacuum, medium vacuum, high vacuum, and ultra-high vacuum. Low vacuum blowers are capable of creating a vacuum level of around 10 to 75 kPa, whereas medium vacuum blowers can achieve a higher vacuum level of around 75 to 760 kPa. High vacuum blowers provide an even higher level, typically ranging from 760 to 1 × 10^-3 kPa. Finally, ultra-high vacuum blowers can generate a vacuum level as low as 1 × 10^-3 to 1 × 10^-10 kPa, making them suitable for extremely sensitive applications.</p></p>
