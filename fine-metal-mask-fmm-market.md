<p><p>Fine Metal Mask (FMM) is a technology used in the manufacturing of electronic devices such as smartphones and displays. There are three types of FMM markets based on fabrication methods: etching, electroforming, and multi-material composite method. </p><p>In the etching method, the mask is created by selectively removing metal from a thin film. Electroforming involves depositing a metal layer onto a master template, which is then separated to form the mask. The multi-material composite method combines different materials, such as metal and polymer, to create the mask. These methods enable precise and efficient production of small and intricate electronic components.</p></p>
