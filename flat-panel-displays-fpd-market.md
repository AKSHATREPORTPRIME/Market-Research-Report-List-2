<p><p>Flat panel displays (FPD) are thin, lightweight electronic visual display technologies used in devices such as televisions, computer monitors, and smartphones. The three main types of FPD are Organic Light Emitting Diode Display (OLED), Liquid Crystal Display (LCD), and Plasma Display (PDP). OLED displays use organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is passed through them, delivering vibrant colors and deep blacks. LCD displays use liquid crystals that manipulate light to produce images, whereas PDP displays use small cells of noble gases and phosphors, creating vivid colors through ionized plasma.</p></p>
