<p><p>MIDI cables are used to connect musical instruments, computers, and other audio devices. There are two main types: single cable and multicore cable. Single cable MIDI connections use one cable to transmit MIDI data between devices. Multicore cables, on the other hand, have multiple cables bundled together in a single sleeve. This allows for more efficient and organized cable management when connecting multiple MIDI devices. Both single cable and multicore cable markets cater to musicians and audio professionals seeking reliable and convenient MIDI connections for their setups.</p></p>
