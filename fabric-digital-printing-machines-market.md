<p><p>Fabric digital printing machines market consists of three types: Sublimation Inkjet Printing, Direct to Fabric Printing, and Direct to Garment Printing. Sublimation Inkjet Printing involves using special inks that convert from a solid to a gas upon heating, which then penetrates the fabric fibers. Direct to Fabric Printing allows digital images to be printed directly onto the fabric, resulting in vibrant colors and sharp details. Direct to Garment Printing enables printing directly onto garments like t-shirts using specialized printers, resulting in highly detailed and durable prints.</p></p>
