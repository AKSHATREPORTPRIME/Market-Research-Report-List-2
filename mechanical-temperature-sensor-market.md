<p><p>The mechanical temperature sensor market consists of two types: contact-type and noncontact-type. Contact-type sensors directly touch the object or medium to measure its temperature. They are commonly used in industries such as automotive and manufacturing. Noncontact-type sensors, on the other hand, measure temperature without physical contact. They work by detecting infrared radiation emitted by the object. These sensors are widely used in applications like medical, aerospace, and food processing industries where direct contact may not be possible or desirable.</p></p>
