<p><p>Workforce management software refers to the tools used by organizations to efficiently manage their workforce. There are two main market types for this software: on-premises and SaaS cloud-based. On-premises software is installed and hosted on the organization's own servers and infrastructure, requiring maintenance and updates by the company's IT department. In contrast, SaaS cloud-based software is hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed via the internet. This type offers scalability, flexibility, and automatic updates, allowing organizations to manage their workforce remotely and focus on their core business activities.</p></p>
