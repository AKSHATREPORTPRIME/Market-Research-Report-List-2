<p><p>The stoma or ostomy care market is divided into two main types: two-piece systems and one-piece systems. Two-piece systems consist of a baseplate and a pouch that can be detached from each other. This allows for easier maintenance and replacement of the pouch while keeping the baseplate intact. On the other hand, one-piece systems have a baseplate and pouch combined into a single unit. These systems are more convenient and suitable for individuals with a well-healed stoma or those who have fewer changes in their stoma size.</p></p>
