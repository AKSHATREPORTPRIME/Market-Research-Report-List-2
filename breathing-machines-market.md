<p><p>Breathing machines are essential medical devices used to assist individuals with respiratory issues. Asthma nebulizers are portable devices that convert liquid medication into a mist, allowing easier inhalation. PAP (positive airway pressure) machines deliver continuous air pressure to keep airways open during sleep for patients with sleep apnea. Oxygen concentrators extract oxygen from the air and provide it to patients with low oxygen levels. Ventilators support individuals unable to breathe properly by delivering oxygen and removing carbon dioxide. These machines collectively cater to various respiratory needs and improve patients' breathing processes.</p></p>
