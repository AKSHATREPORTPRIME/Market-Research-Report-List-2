<p><p>Mould temperature controllers are devices used in the plastic molding industry to regulate and maintain the temperature of the moulds. Oil type controllers function by circulating heated oil into the mould, ensuring precise and consistent temperature control. On the other hand, water type controllers work by circulating heated water into the mould. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the quality and efficiency of moulded plastic products. The choice between oil or water type controllers depends on factors such as desired temperature range, energy efficiency, and specific industry requirements.</p></p>
