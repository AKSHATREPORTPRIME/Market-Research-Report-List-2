<p><p>The pharmaceutical physical testing market offers various types of equipment to assess the characteristics of pharmaceutical products. Hardness testers measure the firmness of tablets, while disintegration testers determine how quickly tablets dissolve. Friability testers gauge the resilience of tablets to abrasion. Tapped density testers assess the packing efficiency of powders, and flowability testers determine the ability of powders to flow. These instruments play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and consistency of pharmaceutical products in terms of durability, dissolution, abrasion resistance, powder density, and flow properties.</p></p>
