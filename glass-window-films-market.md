<p><p>The glass window films market includes various types such as solar control film, security film, decorative film, and spectrally selective film. Solar control films are designed to reduce heat and UV radiation from the sun. Security films provide added strength to glass, making it harder to break and protecting against forced entry. Decorative films are used for privacy or to enhance the aesthetics of glass surfaces. Spectrally selective films allow visible light to enter while blocking heat-causing infrared radiation. These different film types cater to distinct needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
