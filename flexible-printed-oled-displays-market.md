<p><p>Flexible Printed OLED Displays refer to display screens that use organic light-emitting diodes (OLEDs) and are capable of being bent or curved. There are two types of Flexible Printed OLED Displays: Flexible Plastic Based OLED Displays and Flexible Glass Based OLED Displays. Flexible Plastic Based OLED Displays are composed of plastic substrates that provide flexibility and durability. On the other hand, Flexible Glass Based OLED Displays use flexible glass substrates that offer better picture quality and higher resolution. Both types are used in various applications such as smartphones, televisions, and wearable devices.</p></p>
