<p><ul><li>Contact Cards</li><li>Contactless Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic chip cards are broadly classified into two market types: contact cards and contactless cards. Contact cards require physical contact with a card reader or terminal for communication and data transfer. They usually have a metallic chip embedded on the card's surface. On the other hand, contactless cards use radio frequency technology to enable transactions without direct physical contact. They have a chip embedded inside the card, which can be accessed by simply waving the card near a compatible reader or terminal. Both types offer convenient and secure payment options in various industries.</p></p>
