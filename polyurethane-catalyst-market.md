<p><p>The polyurethane catalyst market encompasses three main types: Reactive Amine Catalysts, Non-reactive Amine Catalysts, and Organic Metal Catalysts. Reactive Amine Catalysts are substances that actively participate in the chemical reaction, accelerating the formation of polyurethane. Non-reactive Amine Catalysts, on the other hand, do not participate in the reaction but instead suppress undesired side reactions, improving product quality. Organic Metal Catalysts contain metal ions that promote and control the polyurethane reaction. These different catalyst types play crucial roles in enhancing polyurethane production processes and achieving desired end product characteristics.</p></p>
