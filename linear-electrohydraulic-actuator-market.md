<p><p>Linear electrohydraulic actuators are commonly used in various industries for precise and efficient control of mechanical movement. The market for these actuators can be categorized into two types: control type and switch type. Control type electrohydraulic actuators are designed to provide continuous and adjustable control over the linear movement of a system. On the other hand, switch type electrohydraulic actuators are specifically designed to provide binary control, i.e., enabling or disabling the linear movement of a system. Both types cater to different applications and provide reliable and accurate control functionalities.</p></p>
