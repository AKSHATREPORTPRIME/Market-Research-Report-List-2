<p><p>Pipelayers are essential machines used in the construction industry for the installation of underground pipelines. They are equipped with a hydraulically controlled boom and a large winch that helps in lifting and placing pipeline sections into trenches. Pipelayers are primarily used in oil and gas, water and sewage, and construction industries.</p><p>The pipelayers market is expected to grow at a significant rate during the forecast period. The increasing infrastructure development activities, especially in emerging economies, are driving the demand for pipelayers. The expansion of oil and gas exploration activities, along with the growing need for water and sewage pipelines, is also propelling market growth. Furthermore, advancements in technology have led to the introduction of advanced pipelayers with improved efficiency and productivity, thereby fueling market demand.</p><p>One of the latest trends observed in the pipelayers market is the integration of GPS and telematics technologies. The integration of GPS systems allows operators to precisely position and install pipelines, reducing errors and improving overall efficiency. Telematics solutions enable remote monitoring and tracking of pipelayer machines, helping in preventive maintenance and reducing downtime.</p><p>Another trend is the adoption of automation in pipelayers. Automated pipelayers are equipped with advanced sensors and control systems that enable autonomous operations, reducing the need for manual intervention. This trend is gaining importance due to the shortage of skilled labor in the construction industry.</p><p>In conclusion, the pipelayers market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing infrastructure development activities and advancements in technology. The integration of GPS and telematics technologies, as well as the adoption of automation, are some of the latest trends observed in this market. The market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 14.8% during the forecast period.</p></p>
