<p><p>Current limit switches are used in electrical circuits to protect against excessive current flow. There are two types of current limit switches in the market: fixed and adjustable. Fixed current limit switches have a predefined current threshold that cannot be changed, providing a simple and cost-effective solution for protecting circuits. On the other hand, adjustable current limit switches allow the user to set the desired current threshold, offering more flexibility and customization options. Both types of switches are essential in safeguarding electrical systems from potential damage caused by excessive current.</p></p>
