<p><p>Building-integrated photovoltaic (BIPV) skylights refer to skylights that incorporate solar panels to generate electricity. The market for BIPV skylights can be categorized into two types: crystalline panel and thin film panel. Crystalline panel BIPV skylights utilize silicon-based photovoltaic cells that are more efficient but expensive. Thin film panel BIPV skylights, on the other hand, use a flexible and lightweight film of photovoltaic material, such as amorphous silicon or cadmium telluride. While less efficient, they are cheaper and offer superior design flexibility and aesthetics, easily integrating into various architectural structures.</p></p>
