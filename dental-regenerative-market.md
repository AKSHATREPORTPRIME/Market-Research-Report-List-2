<p><p>Dental regenerative market consists of three main types: Dentin, Dental Pulp, and Tooth Enamel. Dentin refers to the hard tissue beneath the enamel that provides support to the tooth structure. Dental Pulp is the soft connective tissue located in the center of the tooth that contains blood vessels and nerves. It plays a vital role in tooth development and sensation. Tooth Enamel is the tough outer covering of the tooth, which protects it from decay and damage. These three types of dental regenerative markets focus on regenerating and restoring the health and function of the respective tissues in teeth.</p></p>
