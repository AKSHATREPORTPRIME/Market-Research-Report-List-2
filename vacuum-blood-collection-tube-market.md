<p><p>The vacuum blood collection tube market consists of various types, including serum separating tubes, EDTA tubes, plasma separation tubes, and others. Serum separating tubes are specifically designed to separate blood cells from the serum during the centrifugation process. EDTA tubes are used for collecting blood samples for various tests like complete blood count, as EDTA prevents clotting. Plasma separation tubes contain additives that help in separating plasma from blood cells. The "others" category encompasses different types of tubes used for specific purposes like coagulation testing or DNA testing.</p></p>
