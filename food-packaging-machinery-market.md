<p><p>Food Packaging Machinery market can be classified into various types based on their functions. FFS (Form Fill Seal) machines are used to package products by creating a bag from a roll of packaging film, filling it with the product, and sealing it. Labeling and coding machines apply labels and print necessary information (such as expiry dates) on the packaging. Wrapping and bundling machines wrap products in plastic film or bundle them together. Other markets include machinery for cartoning, case packing, and palletizing, which automate the process of placing products into boxes and pallets.</p></p>
