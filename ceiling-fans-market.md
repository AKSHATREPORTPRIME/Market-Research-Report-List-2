<p><p>Ceiling fans are available in two major types: AC ceiling fans and DC ceiling fans. AC ceiling fans operate on alternating current and are the traditional type used in most households. They are affordable and have a wide range of designs and sizes. On the other hand, DC ceiling fans operate on direct current and are more energy-efficient than their AC counterparts. These fans are gaining popularity due to their low power consumption, adjustable speed settings, and quiet operation. While DC ceiling fans are generally more expensive, their long-term energy savings make them a favorable choice for many consumers.</p></p>
