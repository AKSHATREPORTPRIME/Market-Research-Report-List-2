<p><p>Reflective materials are designed to reflect light and increase visibility in low-light conditions. There are two main types in the market: Micro Glass Bead Type Reflective Materials and Micro Prismatic Type Reflective Materials. Micro Glass Bead Type materials consist of tiny glass beads that reflect light back to its source, providing high visibility. On the other hand, Micro Prismatic Type materials use small prisms to reflect light in multiple directions, making them highly effective for enhancing visibility from various angles. Both types play a crucial role in improving safety and are commonly used in traffic signs, clothing, and equipment.</p></p>
