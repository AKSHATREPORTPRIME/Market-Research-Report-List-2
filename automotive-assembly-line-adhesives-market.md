<p><p>The Automotive Assembly Line Adhesives market comprises two main types: Gap Fillers and Structural Adhesives. Gap Fillers are used to fill gaps and spaces between different automotive components, providing insulation, vibration damping, and thermal conductivity. On the other hand, Structural Adhesives are designed to join various parts together, offering high strength and durability. Both types of adhesives play a crucial role in the assembly line process, ensuring the structural integrity and performance of automotive vehicles.</p></p>
