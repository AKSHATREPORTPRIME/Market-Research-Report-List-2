<p><p>Flexible axles are mechanical devices used for transmitting motion or power from one rotating element to another. They consist of a bundle of wires or cables enclosed in a metal or rubber sheath to provide flexibility and torsional movement. They are commonly used in various applications such as automotive, industrial machinery, aerospace, and robotics.</p><p>The flexible axle market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for lightweight and compact power transmission systems. The adoption of flexible axles helps in reducing the overall weight of the machinery, improving fuel efficiency, and enhancing the performance of the equipment.
