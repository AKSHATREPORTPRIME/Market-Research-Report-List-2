<p><strong>The Medium Wave Infrared Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-tube Medium Infrared Heaters</li><li>Twin-tube Medium Infrared Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medium wave infrared heaters are classified into two main types: single-tube medium infrared heaters and twin-tube medium infrared heaters. </p><p>Single-tube medium infrared heaters consist of a single quartz tube that emits medium wave infrared radiation. These heaters are cost-effective and suitable for small to medium-sized spaces. They provide efficient and targeted heating by emitting heat directly towards the desired area.</p><p>On the other hand, twin-tube medium infrared heaters feature two parallel quartz tubes that emit medium wave infrared radiation. These heaters are more powerful and can cover larger areas. They offer uniform heat distribution and are commonly used in commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
