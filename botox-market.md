Botox works by blocking nerve signals in the muscles, leading to temporary muscle paralysis and relaxation.</p><p>The Botox market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 4.9% during the forecast period. Botox is considered a safe and efficient alternative to traditional plastic surgeries.</p><p>Additionally, the rising prevalence of various medical conditions that can be treated with Botox is contributing to market growth. As more healthcare professionals recognize the therapeutic benefits of Botox, its applications are expanding.
