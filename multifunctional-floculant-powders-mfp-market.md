<p><p>Multifunctional Floculant Powders (MFP) are chemical additives that are used in various industries for flocculation purposes. MFPs find applications in water treatment plants, mining, oil and gas, food and beverage, and pharmaceutical industries.</p><p>The current outlook of the Multifunctional Floculant Powders (MFP) market is positive and is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years.
