<p><ul><li>Horizontal Hardening Machine</li><li>Vertical Hardening Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of hardening machines in the market: horizontal hardening machines and vertical hardening machines. Horizontal hardening machines are designed to harden components or parts that have a long length and require uniform hardening along their entire length. On the other hand, vertical hardening machines are suitable for hardening smaller components or parts that have a height or thickness greater than their length. Both types of machines provide the necessary heat treatment to strengthen materials, but they differ in terms of the orientation and size of the parts they can handle.</p></p>
