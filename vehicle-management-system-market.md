<p><p>The Vehicle Management System market can be categorized into two types: Cloud Based and Web Based. Cloud Based Vehicle Management Systems are those that store and process data on remote servers, accessible through the internet. This allows for easy scalability, lower maintenance, and remote access. On the other hand, Web Based Vehicle Management Systems are hosted on a central server and accessed through a web browser. They offer similar benefits as cloud-based systems but may require more maintenance and have limited scalability compared to their cloud-based counterparts.</p></p>
