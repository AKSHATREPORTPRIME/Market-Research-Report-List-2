<p><p>Commercial PV systems refer to solar power systems that are installed on commercial buildings to generate electricity for businesses. There are two main types of commercial PV systems: monocrystalline systems and polysilicon systems. Monocrystalline systems use solar panels made from a single crystal structure, offering higher efficiency and aesthetics but at a higher cost. Polysilicon systems use solar panels made from multiple crystal structures, providing lower efficiency but at a more affordable price. Both types serve the purpose of utilizing solar energy for commercial purposes, but differ in terms of cost and efficiency.</p></p>
