<p><p>Graphite brushes are commonly used in electrical machines and motors. They exist in two main types: artificial graphite and natural graphite. Artificial graphite brushes are created through a manufacturing process using petroleum coke or coal tar pitch as raw materials. They offer improved electrical conductivity and thermal stability. On the other hand, natural graphite brushes are made from mined graphite, offering good lubrication properties and lower friction. Both types have their specific advantages and applications in various industries, such as automotive, aerospace, and power generation.</p></p>
