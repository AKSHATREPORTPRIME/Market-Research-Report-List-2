<p><p>UHV DC Converter Valve market consists of two main types: Flexible DC Converter Valve and Traditional DC Converter Valve. The Flexible DC Converter Valve offers advantages such as higher transmission capacity, improved stability, and flexible power control. It utilizes advanced technologies like voltage-source converter (VSC) and modular multilevel converter (MMC). On the other hand, the Traditional DC Converter Valve employs line-commutated converter (LCC) technology, which has been widely used in the past. However, it lacks the flexibility and control capabilities of the Flexible DC Converter Valve.</p></p>
