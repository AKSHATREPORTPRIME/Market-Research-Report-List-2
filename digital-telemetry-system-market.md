<p><p>Digital Telemetry System Market Types include Single Channel Telemetry and Multi-Channel Telemetry. Single Channel Telemetry refers to a system that transmits data from one remote site to a central location for analysis. It is commonly used for monitoring a specific parameter, such as temperature or pressure. On the other hand, Multi-Channel Telemetry involves the simultaneous transmission of data from multiple remote sites to a central location. This system is suitable for monitoring and analyzing multiple parameters simultaneously, providing a broader view of the overall system.</p></p>
