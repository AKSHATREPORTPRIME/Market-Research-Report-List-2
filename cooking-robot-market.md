<p><strong>The Cooking Robot Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cartesian</li><li>SCARA</li><li>6-Axis</li><li>Cylindrical</li><li>Delta</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cooking robot market is categorized into various types based on their mechanical structure and capabilities. Cartesian robots, also known as gantry robots, have three linear axes and are ideal for handling heavy loads in a precise manner. SCARA robots are known for their selective compliance assembly and are commonly used for assembly tasks. 6-axis robots, similar to human arms, offer a high degree of flexibility and are commonly used for complex tasks. Cylindrical robots have a rotary joint and are suitable for simple assembly tasks. Delta robots have a parallel link structure and are best for high-speed and precision tasks. Lastly, there are other types of cooking robots with unique structures or capabilities that cater to specific applications.</p></p>
