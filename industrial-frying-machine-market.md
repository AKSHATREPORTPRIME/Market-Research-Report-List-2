<p><p>The industrial frying machine market can be classified into two types: batch fryers and continuous fryers. Batch fryers are designed to fry food products in small batches, allowing for more control over the frying process and enabling different products to be fried simultaneously. On the other hand, continuous fryers are designed for high volume production, where food products are continuously fed into the fryer and fried as they pass through the machine. Both types cater to different production needs and offer distinct advantages in terms of flexibility and efficiency.</p></p>
