<p><strong>The Chemisorption Analysis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic Chemisorption Technique</li><li>Static Volumetric Chemisorption Technique</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemisorption analysis is a technique used in surface science to study chemical reactions on a solid surface. The market for chemisorption analysis can be categorized into two types: dynamic chemisorption technique and static volumetric chemisorption technique. </p><p>The dynamic chemisorption technique involves continuously flowing a reactant gas over the solid surface and measuring the adsorption and desorption rates in real-time. This technique provides insights into the kinetics and thermodynamics of the reactions. </p><p>On the other hand, the static volumetric chemisorption technique involves filling a reaction chamber with a reactant gas and allowing it to interact with the solid surface. The change in gas volume is measured to determine the extent of adsorption. This technique provides information about the adsorption capacity and equilibrium of the reaction.</p></p>
