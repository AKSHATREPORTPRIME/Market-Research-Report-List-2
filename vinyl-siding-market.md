<p><p>Vinyl siding is available in two main market types: insulated siding and non-insulated siding. Insulated siding refers to vinyl panels that have an additional layer of insulation, often made from foam, added to the back of the panels. This extra layer of insulation helps improve the energy efficiency of homes by reducing heat loss and increasing thermal resistance. Non-insulated siding, on the other hand, does not have this additional insulation layer and is simpler in design. Both types of vinyl siding offer durability, low maintenance, and a variety of styles and colors to choose from.</p></p>
