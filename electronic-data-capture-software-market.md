<p><p>Electronic Data Capture (EDC) software is used to collect and manage clinical study data in the healthcare industry. The software market can be classified into two types: cloud-based and web-based. Cloud-based EDC software is hosted on remote servers, accessed through the internet, and provides scalability, flexibility, and security. Web-based EDC software is hosted on the user's local servers and requires a dedicated network. Both types offer benefits such as real-time data access, data validation, and efficient data management, but differ in terms of hosting infrastructure and accessibility.</p></p>
