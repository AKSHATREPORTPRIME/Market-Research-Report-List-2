<p><strong>The Medical Glass Bottle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Moulded Bottle</li><li>Tube-Type Bottle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical glass bottle market is categorized into two main types: moulded bottle and tube-type bottle. </p><p>Moulded bottles are produced through the process of moulding molten glass, resulting in a wide range of shapes and sizes. They are preferred for their versatility, strength, and durability. Moulded bottles are commonly used for storing medicines, ointments, and other healthcare products. </p><p>On the other hand, tube-type bottles are made from cylindrical glass tubes. They offer a more standardized shape and are suitable for holding liquid medications, injectables, and laboratory reagents. Tube-type bottles are often chosen for their ease of filling, accurate dosing, and compatibility with various dispensing systems.</p></p>
