<p><p>Hotel housekeeping software market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, allowing hotel staff to access and use the software via the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and eliminates the need for maintenance and infrastructure costs. On the other hand, on-premises software is installed and maintained on the hotel's own servers. It requires IT support, physical infrastructure, and updates. The choice between these types depends on the hotel's specific requirements, budget, and preferences.</p></p>
