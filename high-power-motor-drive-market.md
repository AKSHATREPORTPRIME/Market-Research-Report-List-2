<p><p>The high power motor drive market can be categorized into two types based on voltage levels: high voltage motor drive and middle voltage motor drive. High voltage motor drives are designed to operate at higher voltage levels, typically above 1 kV, and are commonly used in industrial applications that require high power output. On the other hand, middle voltage motor drives are designed to operate at lower voltage levels, typically between 400 V and 1 kV, and are commonly used in applications such as HVAC systems and pump drives.</p></p>
