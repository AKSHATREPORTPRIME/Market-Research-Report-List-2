<p><strong>The Power Assist Wheelchairs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual Power Assist Wheelchair</li><li>Electric Power Assist Wheelchair</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power assist wheelchairs market is divided into two main types: manual power assist wheelchairs and electric power assist wheelchairs. Manual power assist wheelchairs are designed for individuals who have some upper body strength and can self-propel, but may require assistance on inclines or over longer distances. These wheelchairs have a power pack that can be easily attached or detached to provide an additional boost. Electric power assist wheelchairs, on the other hand, are powered by a battery and are suitable for individuals with limited upper body strength or mobility. They offer full-time power assistance and require less physical effort from the user.</p></p>
