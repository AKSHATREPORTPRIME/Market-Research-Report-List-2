<p><p>The disposable medical PVC gloves market can be categorized into two types: powdered and non-powdered gloves. Powdered gloves are typically coated with cornstarch or other powder to make them easier to put on and remove. These gloves are commonly used in the healthcare industry for general examination purposes. On the other hand, non-powdered gloves do not have any powder coating, making them suitable for individuals with latex allergies or sensitivities. They are often preferred in environments where powder contamination needs to be avoided, such as in cleanrooms or during surgical procedures.</p></p>
