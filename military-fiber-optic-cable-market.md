<p><p>The Military Fiber Optic Cable market comprises two types: single-mode and multimode. Single-mode fiber optics transmit a single beam of light and are used for long-distance military applications, allowing data to travel with minimal loss and in higher fidelity. On the other hand, multimode fiber optics transmit multiple beams of light simultaneously, suited for short-range military applications. The choice between these modes depends on the distance and data transmission requirements of military operations, ensuring reliable and efficient communication within military networks.</p></p>
