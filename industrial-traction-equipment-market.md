<p><p>The Industrial Traction Equipment market consists of three main types: Traction Transformers, Traction Motors, and Traction Converters. Traction Transformers are used to transmit electrical energy to traction motors, which are responsible for converting electrical power into mechanical energy to propel vehicles or industrial equipment. Traction Converters, on the other hand, are used to convert electrical energy from the grid into the appropriate form to power the traction motor. All three components are crucial in enabling the efficient operation of electric vehicles and industrial machinery that rely on electrical power for traction.</p></p>
