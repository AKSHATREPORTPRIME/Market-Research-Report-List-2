<p><p>The pneumatic riveting machine market can be classified into two types: fully automatic riveting machines and semi-automatic riveting machines. Fully automatic riveting machines are designed to perform the entire riveting process independently, from feeding the rivets to drilling holes and setting them in place. On the other hand, semi-automatic riveting machines require some manual intervention, such as placing the rivets in position or activating the machine's functions. Both types of machines are widely used in various industries for efficient and reliable riveting operations.</p></p>
