<p><p>Chain belt conveyors are used for transporting heavy materials or goods in industries. There are two main types of chain belt conveyors: multiflexing chain conveyors and drag chain conveyors. Multiflexing chain conveyors allow for horizontal and vertical flexibility, making them suitable for applications with varying layouts. On the other hand, drag chain conveyors use parallel chains to drag materials along a specified path, which is ideal for conveying bulk materials over long distances. Both types offer efficient and reliable movement of goods, contributing to increased productivity in various industries.</p></p>
