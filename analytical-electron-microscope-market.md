<p><p>The Analytical Electron Microscope (AEM) market encompasses two main types of electron microscopes: the Transmission Electron Microscope (TEM) and the Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM). TEM is used to observe the internal structure of a specimen by transmitting electrons through it, providing high-resolution images and elemental analysis. SEM, on the other hand, scans the surface of a specimen with a focused beam of electrons, producing detailed 3D images and elemental analysis. Both TEM and SEM have diverse applications in various fields, including materials science, nanotechnology, and biological research.</p></p>
