<p><p>IFF stands for Identification Friend or Foe, which is a system used by military forces to identify friendly entities and distinguish them from potential threats in the battlefield. The IFF system utilizes a combination of transponders and receivers to exchange coded signals between military platforms such as aircraft, ships, and ground vehicles. This system plays a critical role in preventing friendly fire incidents and enabling effective coordination among allied forces.</p><p>The IFF system market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
