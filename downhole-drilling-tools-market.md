It is suitable for drilling in hard formations. On the other hand, the roller cone drill bit has three cones with rows of teeth that crush the rock during drilling. This type of tool is better suited for softer formations. Both types of tools are crucial for efficient and successful drilling in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
