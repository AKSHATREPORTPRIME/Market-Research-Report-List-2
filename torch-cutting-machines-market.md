<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Torch cutting machines are classified into four main types. Manual cutting machines require the operator to manually guide the torch, making it suitable for small-scale operations. Semi-automatic cutting machines have automated features such as torch height control, making them ideal for medium-scale applications. Imitation shape cutting machines are designed to replicate specific shapes repeatedly, providing precision and efficiency. Lastly, CNC cutting machines utilize computer numerical control technology to automate cutting processes entirely, ensuring accuracy and productivity.</p></p>
