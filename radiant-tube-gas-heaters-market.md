<p><p>Radiant tube gas heaters are a type of industrial heating equipment that use infrared radiation to heat a space. These heaters consist of a long tube made of stainless steel or ceramic, inside which a series of small gas burners are installed. The burners heat the tube and emit infrared radiation, which warms objects and people within the vicinity of the heater.</p><p>The radiant tube gas heaters market has been witnessing steady growth due to several factors. Firstly, the growing industrial sector across the globe has led to increased demand for efficient and cost-effective heating solutions. Radiant tube gas heaters are highly efficient and provide uniform heating, making them ideal for industrial spaces such as warehouses, manufacturing facilities, and automotive garages. </p><p>Additionally, the increasing focus on energy conservation and sustainability has contributed to the market growth of radiant tube gas heaters.
