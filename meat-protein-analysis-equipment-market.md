<p><p>The Meat Protein Analysis Equipment Market consists of two types of technology: DEXA Technology and NIR Technology. DEXA (Dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry) is a method that uses X-rays to measure the density and composition of materials, including meat. It provides accurate and precise analysis of meat protein content. NIR (Near-infrared) Technology, on the other hand, uses near-infrared light to determine the chemical composition of materials, including meat protein. It is a non-destructive and rapid method for protein analysis, offering quick and reliable results. Both technologies are widely used in the meat industry for quality control and product development purposes.</p></p>
