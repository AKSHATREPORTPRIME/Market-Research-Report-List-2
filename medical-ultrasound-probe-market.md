<p><strong>The Medical Ultrasound Probe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Type</li><li>Convex Type</li><li>Phased Array Type</li><li>Endocavitary Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Ultrasound Probe Market is segmented into different types based on their specific features and applications:</p><p>1. Linear Type: These probes have a linear array transducer and are primarily used for producing high-resolution images of superficial structures or body parts with a limited depth of penetration.</p><p>2. Convex Type: Also known as curvilinear probes, these have a curved transducer array and are used for abdominal and obstetric imaging, providing wide field-of-view with good depth penetration.</p><p>3. Phased Array Type: These probes consist of multiple small elements, allowing for electronic beam steering, making them suitable for cardiac and vascular imaging.</p><p>4. Endocavitary Type: These probes are designed for internal examinations and are mostly used in transvaginal, rectal, or transesophageal imaging.</p><p>5. Others: This category comprises specialty ultrasound probes with unique features for specific applications, such as 3D or 4D imaging, intracavity probes, and intraoperative probes.</p></p>
