<p><ul><li>Spiral Mixer</li><li>Planetary Mixer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The baker mixer market consists of different types of mixers, namely spiral mixers and planetary mixers. Spiral mixers are designed with spiral-shaped agitators that rotate the dough in a spiral motion, making them ideal for heavy dough mixing. They are commonly used in commercial bakeries that produce large quantities of bread and pizza dough. On the other hand, planetary mixers have a stationary bowl and a rotating agitator that moves in a planetary or orbital motion. They are versatile and widely used in bakeries for preparing various recipes, including dough, batters, and creams.</p></p>
