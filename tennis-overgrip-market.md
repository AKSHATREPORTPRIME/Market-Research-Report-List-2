<p><p>A tennis overgrip is a thin, cushioned tape that is wrapped around the handle of a tennis racket to provide additional grip and comfort. Overgrips are designed to absorb sweat and moisture, ensuring a firm grip on the racket handle and reducing the chances of the racket slipping during gameplay.</p><p>The tennis overgrip market is experiencing steady growth due to the increasing popularity of tennis as a recreational sport and the growing number of tennis tournaments worldwide. Additionally, the rising awareness among players about the importance of grip control and the need to replace overgrips regularly has further contributed to market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the tennis overgrip market is the introduction of innovative and technologically advanced materials. Manufacturers are incorporating materials that offer enhanced grip, durability, and sweat absorption properties into their overgrips.
