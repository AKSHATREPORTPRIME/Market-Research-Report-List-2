<p><p>Robusta beans are categorized into three market types based on the degree of roasting they undergo: Light Bake, Medium Bake, and Deep Bake. Light Bake Robusta beans are roasted for a shorter time, resulting in a milder flavor profile with more acidity and brightness. Medium Bake Robusta beans are roasted slightly longer, offering a balanced taste between acidity and bitterness. Deep Bake Robusta beans are roasted for the longest duration, producing a bold and robust flavor with lower acidity. These market types enable buyers to choose from various flavor profiles when purchasing Robusta beans.</p></p>
