<p><p>Preset resistors refer to a type of electronic component used to vary the amount of current flowing in a circuit. They are available in two main market types: single turn and multi turn. Single turn preset resistors are designed to be adjusted by rotating a dial or screw once for varying resistance values. Both types are vital in electronic applications where precise and adjustable resistance settings are needed for optimal circuit performance.</p></p>
