<p><p>Solar pumps are used for various applications, and there are two main types available in the market: submersible pumps and surface pumps. Submersible pumps are installed underwater and are commonly used for agriculture and irrigation, as they can pump water from wells, boreholes, or reservoirs. On the other hand, surface pumps are installed above the ground and are primarily used for domestic water supply or livestock watering. They draw water from sources such as rivers, lakes, or shallow wells. Both types of solar pumps are powered by solar panels, making them eco-friendly and cost-effective alternatives to conventional pumps.</p></p>
