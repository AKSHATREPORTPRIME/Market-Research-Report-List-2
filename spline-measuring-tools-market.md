<p><p>The spline measuring tools market encompasses two main types of tools: spline gauge and spline meter. A spline gauge is a precision instrument used to measure the dimensions and geometry of a spline, ensuring accurate fit and alignment. It helps in ensuring the proper functioning of machines with splined components. On the other hand, a spline meter is a handheld device that measures the pitch, diameter, and other parameters of a spline. It is commonly used in industries like automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing to inspect and measure the quality of splined parts.</p></p>
