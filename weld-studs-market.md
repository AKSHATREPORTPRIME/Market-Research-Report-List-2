<p><p>Weld Studs market includes different types such as Manual Welding, Arc Welding, and Energy Storage Welding. Manual Welding involves the process of attaching the stud to a workpiece manually using a welding gun. Arc Welding utilizes an electric arc to generate heat and create a joint. Energy Storage Welding involves using stored energy, like capacitors, to release a high amount of energy for welding. These various types offer different methods and techniques for effectively and efficiently welding studs onto workpieces.</p></p>
