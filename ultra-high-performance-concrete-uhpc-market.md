It is used in various construction applications that require superior performance. The UHPC market includes several types such as SIFCON (Slurry Infiltrated Fibrous Concrete), Reactive Powder Concrete (RPC), Compact Reinforced Composite (CRC), and Others. SIFCON involves the infiltration of fibrous preforms by cementitious slurry, while RPC is characterized by the use of very fine powders with high cementitious content. CRC utilizes short steel fibers to improve ductility, strength, and durability. Other types refer to additional UHPC variations developed by researchers and manufacturers.</p></p>
