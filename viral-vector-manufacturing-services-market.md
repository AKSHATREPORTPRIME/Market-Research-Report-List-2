<p><p>The viral vector manufacturing services market includes various types of viral vectors used in gene therapy and vaccine development. Retroviral vectors are derived from retroviruses and are effective for stable gene transfer. Adenoviral vectors are based on adenoviruses and are commonly used for gene delivery in both research and clinical applications. Adeno-associated viral vectors are derived from adeno-associated viruses and are popular due to their low immunogenicity. Other viral vectors include lentiviral vectors, herpes simplex virus vectors, and vaccinia virus vectors, each with unique advantages and applications in gene therapy and vaccine development.</p></p>
