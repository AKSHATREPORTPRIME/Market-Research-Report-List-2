<p><p>Medical Grade Polyurethane (PU) is a versatile material used in various medical applications. In the market, it is divided into different types: foams, elastomers, films, and others. PU foams are used for cushioning, wound dressings, and orthopedic devices. Elastomers provide flexibility and durability, used in catheters, tubing, and seals. PU films are used for surgical drapes, medical packaging, and transdermal drug delivery. Other applications include adhesives, coatings, and gels. Each type offers specific benefits, such as biocompatibility, resistance to chemicals, and ease of sterilization, making them crucial in the medical industry.</p></p>
