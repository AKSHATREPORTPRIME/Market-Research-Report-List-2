<p><p>Appliance automation robots are classified into different market types based on their capabilities and movement axes. The 4-axis robots have four degrees of freedom and can move in three axes for reaching and rotating tasks. The 5-axis robots add an additional rotating axis for increased flexibility in various applications. 6-axis robots offer superior dexterity as they can move in six axes, enabling them to perform complex tasks with precision. The 7-axis robots consist of an additional wrist axis with enhanced flexibility for handling irregular or challenging objects. Lastly, the "Other" market includes robots with unconventional or specialized configurations catering to specific industry requirements.</p></p>
