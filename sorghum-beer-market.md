<p><ul><li>Wet-based</li><li>Dry-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sorghum beer is classified into two market types based on the production process: wet-based and dry-based. In the wet-based market, sorghum grains are soaked in water to facilitate germination, which will convert the starches into sugars. The germinated grains are then milled, mixed with water, and fermented to produce sorghum beer. On the other hand, in the dry-based market, the sorghum grains are milled without undergoing the germination process. The milled grains are mixed with water and fermented to produce sorghum beer.</p></p>
