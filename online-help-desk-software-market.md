<p><p>Cloud-based and web-based help desk software are two types of tools used for managing customer support and technical assistance. Cloud-based help desk software operates on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage customer support functionalities through the internet. This ensures flexibility and easy scalability as the software is accessible from any device with internet access. On the other hand, web-based help desk software is installed and hosted on local servers, limiting access to the organizations' internal network. However, both types provide similar functionalities, such as ticket management, knowledge base, and customer communication, improving overall customer service efficiency.</p></p>
