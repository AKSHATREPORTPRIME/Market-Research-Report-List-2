<p><p>In the conductivity probe market, there are various types of probes available, including ring probe, pen probe, and needle probe. A ring probe is usually a circular-shaped sensor that can be placed around a conductor to measure its conductivity. A pen probe, as the name suggests, is a pen-like probe with a sensor at the tip, which can be dipped into a substance to measure its conductivity. A needle probe is a thin, pointed probe that can be inserted into a substance or material to measure its conductivity. These different types of probes offer versatility in conductivity measurements for different applications.</p></p>
