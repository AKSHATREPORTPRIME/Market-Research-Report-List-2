<p><p>Yogurt powders are categorized into three types: Skimmed Yogurt Powder, Semi-Skimmed Yogurt Powder, and Whole Yogurt Powder. Skimmed Yogurt Powder is made from skimmed milk, which has had most of its fat content removed. Semi-Skimmed Yogurt Powder is made from partially skimmed milk, where some of the fat is removed but not as much as in skimmed milk. Whole Yogurt Powder is made from whole milk, containing the full fat content. These different types cater to varying consumer preferences and dietary needs, providing options for both low-fat and full-fat yogurt powder products.</p></p>
