<p><p>The intercoms market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless. Wired intercoms require physical cables to connect the various components of the system, allowing for uninterrupted communication. This type is commonly found in buildings that already have wiring infrastructure installed. On the other hand, wireless intercoms rely on radio communication technology to transmit signals and enable communication between different devices. This type offers more flexibility and is suitable for places where wiring installation is difficult or not desired, such as outdoor areas or temporary locations.</p></p>
