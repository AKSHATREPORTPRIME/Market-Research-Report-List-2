<p><p>Heat sinks and cooling fins are components used in electronic devices to dissipate heat and keep them operating at optimal temperatures. Heat sinks are passive cooling devices made of metals, such as aluminum or copper, with a large surface area to absorb and dissipate heat through conduction, convection, and radiation. Cooling fins are smaller and thinner, often attached to heat sinks, providing additional surface area to enhance heat dissipation.</p><p>The heat sinks and cooling fins market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for electronic devices like smartphones, tablets, and gaming consoles, which generate high levels of heat, is a driving factor for market growth.
