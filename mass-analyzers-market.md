<p><p>Mass analyzers are widely used in various fields such as pharmaceuticals, proteomics, and environmental analysis. The quadrupole mass analyzer is a widely adopted type that separates ions based on their mass-to-charge ratio. Time of Flight mass analyzers measure an ion's time it takes to travel a specific distance, providing accurate mass analysis. The magnetic sector mass analyzer uses a magnetic field to bend ions, separating them based on their mass. Electrostatic sector mass analyzers rely on electric fields to deflect ions. Other types include FTMS and ion trap analyzers.</p></p>
