<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Video Display Controllers(VDC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Video Shifters</li><li>Cathode Ray Tube Controllers</li><li>Video Interface Controllers</li><li>Video Coprocessors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Video Display Controllers (VDCs) are electronic devices that manage the visual output of video signals to display devices. There are several types of VDCs found in the market. Video Shifters are responsible for converting digital video signals to analog signals compatible with displays. Cathode Ray Tube (CRT) Controllers handle video signals specifically for CRT displays. Video Interface Controllers connect video sources to displays via different interfaces. Lastly, Video Coprocessors specialize in handling complex video processing tasks, such as video compression or encoding. These VDCs play an essential role in delivering high-quality visuals on various display devices.</p></p>
