<p><p>The Vinyl Sulphone Ester market consists of three types: Para Base Ester, OA Base, and Para Cresidine Base. Para Base Ester is derived from para-aminobenzoic acid and is mainly used in the textile industry for dyeing purposes. OA Base, also known as Ortho Anisidine Base, is used as a raw material for the production of Vinyl Sulphone Ester and other dyes. Lastly, Para Cresidine Base is synthesized from p-cresidine and finds applications in the production of reactive dyes and pigments. These market types cater to specific industry needs within the Vinyl Sulphone Ester market.</p></p>
