<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The photovoltaic inverter market is categorized into three main types: string inverters, central inverters, and microinverters. String inverters are commonly used in large-scale solar installations and connect multiple solar panels in series. Central inverters are typically used in utility-scale solar power plants and handle high voltage inputs. Meanwhile, microinverters are installed on each solar panel, converting DC power to AC power individually. This type allows for optimization of energy production and offers increased system performance.</p></p>
