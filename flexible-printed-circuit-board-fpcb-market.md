<p><p>Flexible Printed Circuit Board (FPCB) market types include Single Sided Flex Circuits, Double Sided Flex Circuits, Multi-Layer Flex Circuits, Rigid Flex Circuits, and Others. Single Sided Flex Circuits consist of one conductive layer on a flexible substrate. Double Sided Flex Circuits have conductive layers on both sides of the substrate. Multi-Layer Flex Circuits have multiple layers of conductive material sandwiched between flexible substrates. Rigid Flex Circuits combine rigid and flexible substrates to offer a combination of both properties. Other FPCB types encompass various specialized designs, including stacked and sculpted flex circuits, depending on specific applications.</p></p>
