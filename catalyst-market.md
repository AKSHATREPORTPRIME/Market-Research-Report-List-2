<p><p>Catalyst market types can be categorized into various groups. Zeolites are crystalline aluminosilicate compounds widely used in industries. Metals such as platinum and palladium are utilized as catalysts due to their properties. Chemical compounds like oxides or sulfides of metals are used to catalyze specific reactions. Enzymes, which are biological catalysts, are employed in industries for their efficiency and specificity. Organometallic materials, combining organic and metal components, find applications as catalysts in various chemical processes. These different catalyst market types play crucial roles in accelerating chemical reactions and improving industrial processes.</p></p>
