<p><strong>The Ultrafine Zinc Oxide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Process (America Method)</li><li>Indirect Process (French Method)</li><li>Wet Chemical Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrafine zinc oxide market can be categorized into three types: direct process (American Method), indirect process (French Method), and wet chemical process. Direct process involves the reduction of zinc with a carbon source at high temperatures, resulting in ultrafine zinc oxide powder. Indirect process involves the oxidation of metallic zinc vapor over a hot flame, which then forms ultrafine zinc oxide particles. Wet chemical process involves the dissolution of zinc salts in a solution, followed by precipitation and drying to obtain ultrafine zinc oxide powder.</p></p>
