<p><ul><li>Dry Separator</li><li>Wet Separator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sodium-ion battery market comprises two types of separators: dry separator and wet separator. A dry separator is a porous membrane that prevents direct contact between the positive and negative electrodes, enabling ion movement while maintaining the electrolyte's stability. Conversely, a wet separator is soaked in the electrolyte solution, allowing for improved ion conductivity. The choice between these separators depends on factors like battery performance, cost, and application requirements. Both separators play a critical role in optimizing the efficiency and longevity of sodium-ion batteries.</p></p>
