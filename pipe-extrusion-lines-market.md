<p><p>Pipe extrusion lines are machines used in the manufacturing of different types of pipes, such as PVC, HDPE, and PE. These lines include various components like an extruder, die head, calibration unit, cooling system, haul-off unit, and cutting unit. The extruder melts the raw material, which is then pushed through the die head to obtain the desired pipe shape and size. The calibration unit ensures uniformity and precision, while the cooling system cools down the pipe to solidify it. The haul-off and cutting units further shape and cut the pipe into desired lengths.</p><p>The pipe extrusion lines market is expected to witness a steady growth rate in the coming years. <p><p>Pipe extrusion lines are used in the manufacturing of pipes of various sizes and materials. There are two main types of pipe extrusion lines: vertical and horizontal. </p><p>Vertical type pipe extrusion lines have a vertical configuration, where the extruder is positioned above the die and the pipe is extruded vertically downwards. This design allows for a compact layout and efficient space utilization.</p><p>Horizontal type pipe extrusion lines, on the other hand, have a horizontal configuration, with the extruder and die positioned on the same level. This design allows for easy accessibility, maintenance, and operation.</p><p>Both types of pipe extrusion lines have their own advantages and are used in different applications based on the production requirements and available space.</p></p>
