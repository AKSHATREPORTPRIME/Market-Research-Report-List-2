<p><p>Co-current flow dryers are used in various industries for removing moisture from a product. There are two main types of co-current flow dryers: vertical dryers and horizontal dryers. Vertical dryers are characterized by a tall, cylindrical design where the product is fed from the top and the hot air flows in the same direction towards the bottom. Horizontal dryers, on the other hand, have a rectangular design where the product is fed from one end and the hot air flows parallel to the product until it reaches the opposite end. Both types offer efficient drying solutions depending on the specific requirements of the industry.</p></p>
