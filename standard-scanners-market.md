<p><p>Standard scanners are divided into two main market types: flatbed scanners and feed-through scanners. Flatbed scanners are commonly used for home or small office purposes. They consist of a flat glass surface on which the document is placed for scanning. Feed-through scanners, also known as automatic document feeders (ADFs), are designed for high-volume document scanning. They allow multiple documents to be loaded into a tray and scanned automatically one after another. These scanners are typically used in larger organizations where efficiency and speed are crucial.</p></p>
