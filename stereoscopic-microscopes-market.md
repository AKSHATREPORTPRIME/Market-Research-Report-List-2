<p><p>Stereoscopic microscopes are available in two main types: binocular and trinocular. Binocular microscopes have two eyepieces, allowing for comfortable viewing with both eyes and providing a three-dimensional image of the sample. Trinocular microscopes, on the other hand, have three eyepieces - two for observation and one for attaching a camera or other imaging device. This enables the users to capture or record images or videos of the observed specimen. Both types serve as essential tools in fields like biology, microscopy, and research.</p></p>
