<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Automatic Mercury Analyzer market consists of two types: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer and Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer. The former type utilizes the cold vapor technique to separate and measure elemental mercury present in samples. It quantifies the mercury concentration based on the absorption of light. The latter type employs the cold atomic fluorescence technique, which involves the excitation of mercury atoms to emit light, enabling the measurement of mercury concentration. Both technologies play a crucial role in various industries for accurate mercury analysis and environmental monitoring purposes.</p></p>
