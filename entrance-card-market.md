<p><p>The entrance card market can be categorized into contact type and contactless type based on their functionality. Contact type entrance cards require physical contact with a reader to transmit information, such as using a magnetic strip or inserting a chip. On the other hand, contactless type entrance cards enable information transfer without direct physical contact, using technologies like radio frequency identification (RFID) or near-field communication (NFC). Both market types serve the purpose of accessing secured areas or facilities, but contactless cards offer a more convenient and efficient user experience.</p></p>
