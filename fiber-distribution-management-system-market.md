<p><p>A Fiber Distribution Management System (FDMS) is utilized in both Singlemode Fiber and Multimode Fiber markets. In the Singlemode Fiber market, FDMS is employed for managing the distribution and organization of optical fibers that support long-distance, high-bandwidth communications. Similarly, in the Multimode Fiber market, FDMS is used to manage the distribution and organization of optical fibers that facilitate shorter distance, high-capacity data transmissions. The purpose of FDMS in both markets is to ensure efficient and reliable connectivity by consolidating, protecting, and providing accessibility to fiber optic cables.</p></p>
