<p><p>Monoclonal antibody therapeutics market includes various types, such as humanized antibodies and human mouse chimeric antibodies. Humanized antibodies are artificial antibodies created by modifying non-human antibodies to resemble human antibodies. They possess a small portion of non-human sequence along with a majority of human sequence. On the other hand, human mouse chimeric antibodies are generated by combining genetic material from both human and mouse antibodies. These types of monoclonal antibodies have revolutionized the field of therapeutics, offering potential treatments for various diseases like cancer and autoimmune disorders.</p></p>
