<p><p>The portable water quality meters market includes various types of meters used to measure different parameters in water. pH meters are used to determine the level of acidity or alkalinity. Conductivity meters measure the ability of water to conduct an electric current. Chlorine meters are used to measure the concentration of chlorine in water. ORP meters determine the oxidation-reduction potential of water. Dissolved oxygen meters measure the amount of oxygen dissolved in water. Turbidity meters assess the clarity of water. Salinity meters measure the salt content in water. Other meters may include those used to measure specific contaminants or parameters in water.</p></p>
