<p><p>Semiconductor Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) Equipment is used in the semiconductor industry to deposit thin films on semiconductor wafers with atomic precision. There are two main types of ALD equipment: Thermal ALD Equipment and Plasma-Enhanced ALD (PEALD) Equipment. Thermal ALD equipment relies on thermal reactions to deposit films, while PEALD equipment uses plasma to enhance the deposition process. Both types play a crucial role in the fabrication of advanced semiconductor devices, offering precise control over film thickness, conformality, and material properties.</p></p>
