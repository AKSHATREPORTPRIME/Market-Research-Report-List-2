Standalone scanners are compact and have their own built-in display. Wall-mounted scanners are fixed to the wall, providing convenience and space-saving. Portable scanners, as the name suggests, are lightweight and can be easily moved around. Each type of scanner offers its own advantages and is suited for different dental practices.</p></p>
