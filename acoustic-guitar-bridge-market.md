<p><p>The acoustic guitar bridge market is divided into two main types: pin bridge and pinless bridge. The pin bridge design utilizes bridge pins to secure the strings to the bridge plate, creating a traditional and popular bridge style. On the other hand, pinless bridges do not require bridge pins and instead use a different mechanism to hold the strings in place. While both types serve the same function of connecting the strings to the body of the guitar, they differ in their design and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
