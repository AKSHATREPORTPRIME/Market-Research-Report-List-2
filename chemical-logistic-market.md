<p><p>Chemical logistics involve the transportation of chemicals from one location to another. There are several market types in this industry. Pipelines transport involves the use of pipes to transport chemicals over long distances efficiently. Rail transport utilizes trains to move chemicals across the country, providing a cost-effective option. Road transport employs trucks for shorter distance shipments, offering flexibility and door-to-door delivery. Intermodal transport combines multiple modes, such as rail and truck, for efficient and flexible transportation. Sea transport involves the use of ships to move chemicals globally, ensuring large volume shipments and cost savings.</p></p>
