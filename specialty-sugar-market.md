<p><p>Specialty sugars are categorized into various types based on their unique characteristics and usage in the market. Cinnamon sugar is a blend of sugar and cinnamon, commonly used to enhance the flavor of baked goods and beverages. Baked sugar has larger crystals, making it ideal for a crunchy texture in pastries and desserts. Black onyx sugar is unrefined and has a rich molasses flavor, perfect for indulgent treats. Pure maple sugar is derived from maple sap and adds a natural sweetness to recipes. Breakfast sugar is specially designed for sprinkling on cereals and fruits. Castor sugar is finely granulated and dissolves quickly, suitable for baking delicate goods. Other types include powdered sugar, muscovado sugar, and demerara sugar, each serving unique purposes in the culinary world.</p></p>
