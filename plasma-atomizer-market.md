<p><p>Plasma atomization refers to a process where a plasma jet is used to melt and vaporize a material, resulting in the production of fine particles. Plasma rotating electrode process atomization, on the other hand, involves the use of a rotating electrode immersed in a plasma jet to melt and atomize the material. Both methods are utilized in the production of powders with desired characteristics, such as high purity and controlled particle size, and find application in various industries including aerospace, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
