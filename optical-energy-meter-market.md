<p><p>The optical energy meter market encompasses various types of meters used to measure optical energy. Pyroelectric energy meters utilize the heat generated by absorbed radiation to determine energy levels. Photodiode energy meters rely on the electrical current produced when light is absorbed by a semiconductor. Other types could include thermal detectors, which measure temperature changes caused by radiation, or integrating sphere meters that capture and measure scattered or transmitted light. These different technologies offer options for accurately measuring and quantifying optical energy in various applications and industries.</p></p>
