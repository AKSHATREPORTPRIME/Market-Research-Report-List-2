<p><p>The shared motorcycle market can be classified into two types: no deposit and deposit type markets. In the no deposit market, users can rent motorcycles without providing any upfront deposit. Conversely, in the deposit type market, users are required to pay a certain amount as a deposit upfront. This is typically preferred by occasional riders who may want to secure a motorcycle for longer periods or need access to additional features.</p></p>
