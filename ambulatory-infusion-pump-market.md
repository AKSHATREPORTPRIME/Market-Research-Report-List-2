<p><p>The ambulatory infusion pump market consists of two main types: mechanical pumps and electronic pumps. Mechanical pumps operate using a mechanical mechanism to deliver medication to patients in a controlled manner. These pumps are usually simple to operate and maintain. On the other hand, electronic pumps are more technologically advanced and allow for more precise control and monitoring of the infusion process. They typically feature programmable settings, alarms, and integration with other medical devices. Both types play a crucial role in providing continuous infusion therapy to patients who require medication outside of a hospital setting.</p></p>
