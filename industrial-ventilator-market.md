<p><p>The Industrial Ventilator market consists of three main types: inhalation, exclusion, and combined. Inhalation ventilators are designed to extract contaminated air and fumes from a specific area, maintaining clean and fresh air quality. Exclusion ventilators, on the other hand, prevent external air pollutants from entering a controlled environment, ensuring a clean and safe workplace. Lastly, combined ventilators provide both inhalation and exclusion functionalities, purifying the air while preventing further contamination. These different market types cater to various industrial settings, offering tailored solutions for specific ventilation needs.</p></p>
