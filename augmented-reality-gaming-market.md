<p><p>The augmented reality gaming market can be categorized into three types based on the display technology used. Head Mounted Display (HMD) involves wearing a device on the user's head, like a VR headset, to immerse them in the virtual world while overlaying digital elements. Handheld Display utilizes mobile devices like smartphones or tablets to superimpose virtual objects onto the real world. Spatial Display employs projector-based systems to project virtual objects onto physical surfaces, providing a more interactive and shared augmented reality gaming experience.</p></p>
