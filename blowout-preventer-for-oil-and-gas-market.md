<p><p>A blowout preventer (BOP) is a crucial component in the oil and gas industry used to prevent uncontrolled releases of oil or gas from a wellbore during drilling or production operations. There are two main types of BOPs: annular blowout preventers and ram blowout preventers. Annular BOPs are designed to seal the wellbore by utilizing a rubber seal, while ram BOPs use two closing rams that clamp together to isolate the wellbore. Both types provide vital safety measures in the oil and gas market, ensuring environmental protection and preventing blowouts.</p></p>
