<p><strong>The Walnut Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold-Pressed Walnut Oil</li><li>Expeller-Pressed Walnut Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Walnut oil is available in two main market types: Cold-Pressed Walnut Oil and Expeller-Pressed Walnut Oil. Cold-pressed walnut oil is extracted by pressing the walnuts using low temperature, which helps retain the natural flavor, aroma, and nutritional value. It is often preferred for its rich nutty taste and high levels of omega-3 fatty acids. On the other hand, expeller-pressed walnut oil is obtained through a mechanical process that involves high pressure and heat. It has a milder flavor and is commonly used for cooking purposes due to its higher smoke point.</p></p>
