<p><strong>The Strain Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Strain Sensor</li><li>Transverse Strain Sensor</li><li>Longitudinal Strain Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The strain sensor market consists of three types: surface strain sensors, transverse strain sensors, and longitudinal strain sensors. Surface strain sensors are used to measure the strain on the surface of an object, providing information on its deformation. Transverse strain sensors measure the strain perpendicular to the length of an object, while longitudinal strain sensors measure the strain along the length. These different types of strain sensors are used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and healthcare for applications such as structural health monitoring and non-destructive testing.</p></p>
