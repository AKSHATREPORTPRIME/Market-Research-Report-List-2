<p><p>The hollow sucker rod market is categorized into three types: C Grade, D Grade, and H Grade. C Grade rods are made from carbon steel and are commonly used in shallow well applications. D Grade rods are also made from carbon steel but have higher tensile strength compared to C Grade, making them suitable for deeper well operations. H Grade rods, on the other hand, are made from alloy steel and offer the highest tensile strength, making them ideal for extremely deep well drilling.</p></p>
