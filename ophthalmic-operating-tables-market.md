<p><p>Ophthalmic operating tables are essential equipment used during eye surgeries. There are three main types available in the market: electric operating tables, hydraulic operating tables, and electro-hydraulic operating tables. Electric operating tables are powered by electricity and offer precise movements and positioning. Hydraulic operating tables use hydraulic systems to control the table's movements and can support heavy loads. Electro-hydraulic operating tables combine features of both electric and hydraulic tables, providing precise positioning and the ability to lift heavy loads efficiently. Each type has its distinct advantages and is suitable for different surgical requirements.</p></p>
