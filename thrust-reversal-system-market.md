<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Thrust Reversal System market consists of three main types: the Target Type, Clam-shell Type, and Cold stream Type. Target Type Thrust Reversal System redirects the airflow by deploying blocker doors onto the engine nacelle. Clam-shell Type Thrust Reversal System incorporates two clamshell doors that open outward to deflect the exhaust gases. Cold stream Type Thrust Reversal System redirects the engine exhaust by diverting it into an external stream of air called the cold stream. These types offer different mechanisms to effectively reverse thrust and enhance braking efficiency in aircraft.</p></p>
