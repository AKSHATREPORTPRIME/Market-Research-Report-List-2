<p><p>Defatted Fish Meal is categorized into two main types: Flame Dried (FD) Fish Meal and Steam Dried (SD) Fish Meal. Flame Dried Fish Meal is produced by exposing the fish to high heat through direct flame contact, resulting in a dry and powdery texture. On the other hand, Steam Dried Fish Meal is made by using steam to remove the moisture from the fish. This process retains more nutrients and results in a lighter-colored meal. Both types of fish meal are widely used in various industries such as animal feed, aquaculture, and pet food.</p></p>
