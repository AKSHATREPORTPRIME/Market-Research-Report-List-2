<p><p>Material handling equipment refers to various types of machinery and devices used to move, store, and transport materials within a manufacturing or distribution facility. There are four main types: storage and handling equipment, industrial trucks, bulk material handling equipment, and automated storage and retrieval systems (AS/RS). Storage and handling equipment includes shelving, racks, and cabinets. Industrial trucks, such as forklifts and pallet jacks, are used for lifting and transporting materials. Bulk material handling equipment is designed for handling large quantities of materials, such as conveyors and cranes. AS/RS is a computerized system for storing and retrieving materials efficiently.</p></p>
