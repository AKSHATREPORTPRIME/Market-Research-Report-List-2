<p><p>Level gauges are devices used to measure and monitor the levels of liquids or fluids in tanks or containers. The market for level gauges can be categorized into three types: visual, electronic, and others. Visual level gauges utilize clear tubes or sight glasses and provide a direct visual indicator of the fluid level. Electronic level gauges use sensors, transmitters, or probes to measure the level and transmit the data electronically. The "others" category includes non-traditional level gauges like ultrasonic or radar devices that use advanced technologies for accurate level measurement.</p></p>
