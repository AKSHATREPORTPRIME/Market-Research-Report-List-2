<p><strong>The Inland Marine Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free from Particular Average</li><li>with Particular Average</li><li>All Risk</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inland Marine Insurance market types can be broadly classified into three categories: Free from Particular Average, with Particular Average, and All Risk. Free from Particular Average policy covers losses or damages only if they occur due to specified perils like fire or theft, excluding ordinary risks such as breakage. With Particular Average policy covers losses or damages caused by specified perils and partially covers ordinary risks. All Risk policy offers coverage for losses or damages caused by all perils except for those explicitly excluded. Other markets in the Inland Marine Insurance include policies for specific items or industries such as fine arts, jewelers block, or bailees.</p></p>
