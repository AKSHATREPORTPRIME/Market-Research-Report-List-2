<p><p>Cancer gene therapy is a type of treatment that involves the delivery of genes into a patient's body to treat cancer. The market growth is primarily driven by the increasing prevalence of cancer worldwide and the growing demand for targeted and personalized therapies. Additionally, advancements in gene therapy technologies and the increasing number of clinical trials are further driving market growth.</p><p>The market is witnessing several trends that are shaping its growth. One such trend is the development of gene therapy products targeting specific genetic mutations or alterations in cancer cells.
