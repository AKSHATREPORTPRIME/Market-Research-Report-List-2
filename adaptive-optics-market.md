<p><p>The Adaptive Optics market consists of three major types: Wavefront Sensor, Wavefront Modulator, and Control System. A wavefront sensor measures deviations in light waves to create an accurate reference for the system, ensuring precise imaging. Wavefront modulators manipulate incoming light waves to compensate for distortions in real-time, resulting in improved image quality. The control system oversees the entire adaptive optics process, synchronizing the sensor and modulator to achieve optimal performance. These components work together to correct distortions caused by atmospheric turbulence, enabling clearer and more detailed imaging in various applications such as astronomy, microscopy, and laser communication.</p></p>
