<p><p>Cryogenic strainers are used in industrial applications to remove impurities from fluids at ultra-low temperatures. They come in various types such as flanged, welded, threaded, and others. Flanged cryogenic strainers have flanges at both ends for easy installation and removal. Welded cryogenic strainers are fixed directly into the pipeline using welding techniques, making them a more permanent solution. Threaded cryogenic strainers feature threaded ends for convenient installation on threaded pipelines. "Others" refers to cryogenic strainers with unique configurations or features that do not fit into the other three categories.</p></p>
