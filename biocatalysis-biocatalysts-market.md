The market for biocatalysis is categorized into different types based on the enzymes involved. Hydrolases are enzymes that facilitate the breakdown of chemical bonds through the addition of water. Oxidoreductases are enzymes that catalyze oxidation-reduction reactions. Transferases are enzymes that transfer functional groups between molecules. The "Others" category includes enzymes such as ligases and isomerases that have unique catalytic functions. Each of these types plays a crucial role in the biocatalysis market.</p></p>
