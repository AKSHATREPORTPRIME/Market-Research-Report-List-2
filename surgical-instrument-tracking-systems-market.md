<p><p>Surgical instrument tracking systems are technologically advanced systems designed to track and trace surgical instruments throughout their lifecycle, from sterile processing departments to operating rooms. The market is primarily driven by the increasing adoption of these systems by healthcare facilities to overcome the challenges associated with manual tracking, such as errors, inefficiencies, and the risk of instrument loss or misplacement. The need for automation and digitization in the healthcare sector is also contributing to market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising number of surgical procedures, the increasing focus on reducing healthcare-associated infections (HAIs), and the growing demand for efficient inventory management systems are propelling the demand for surgical instrument tracking systems.
