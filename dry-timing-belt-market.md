<p><p>The dry timing belt market consists of various types of components that are essential for its proper functioning. Tensioners help maintain the appropriate tension in the belt, ensuring efficient power transfer. Idler pulleys guide the belt and reduce vibration, improving overall performance. Timing shields/covers protect the belt from external damage and contaminants. Sprockets, on the other hand, engage with the belt's teeth, creating rotational movement. All these components are crucial for maintaining the integrity and durability of a dry timing belt system.</p></p>
