<p><strong>The Copper Wire Bonding ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball-Ball Bonds</li><li>Wedge-Wedge Bonds</li><li>Ball-Wedge Bonds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Copper wire bonding is a method used to connect integrated circuits (ICs) to their package substrates. There are different types of copper wire bonding techniques in the market. Ball-ball bonds involve creating a spherical ball shape at the ends of the wire and connecting them to specific points on the IC and substrate. Wedge-wedge bonds use a wedge-shaped tool to bond the wire to the IC's pads. Lastly, ball-wedge bonds combine the ball and wedge techniques to create a ball shape on one end and a wedge shape on the other.</p></p>
