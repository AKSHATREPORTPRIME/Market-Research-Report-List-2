<p><ul><li>Straight Filling Machine</li><li>Spiral Filling Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The premade pouch filling machine market consists of two main types: straight filling machines and spiral filling machines. Straight filling machines are designed to fill premade pouches in a linear and uninterrupted manner. They are suitable for products that require precise filling and involve liquid or viscous substances. On the other hand, spiral filling machines use a rotating spiral motion to fill premade pouches with products that require a more gentle handling, such as fragile or delicate items. Both types cater to the diverse needs of the premade pouch packaging industry.</p></p>
