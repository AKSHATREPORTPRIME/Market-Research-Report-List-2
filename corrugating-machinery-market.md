<p><ul><li>Wet End Equipment</li><li>Dry End Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The corrugating machinery market is divided into two types: wet end equipment and dry end equipment. Wet end equipment refers to the machinery used in the initial stages of the corrugating process, which includes the corrugating rolls, preheaters, and glue units. It ensures the proper formation and application of the corrugating medium. On the other hand, dry end equipment includes machines like slitters, cutters, and stackers that are used in the final stages of the process to cut, stack, and package the corrugated sheets. This division enables efficient and smooth operation throughout the entire corrugating process.</p></p>
