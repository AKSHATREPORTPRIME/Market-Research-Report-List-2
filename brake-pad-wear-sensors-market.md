<p><p>The Brake Pad Wear Sensors market comprises two main types: Electronic Pad Wear Sensors and Audible Pad Wear Sensors. Electronic sensors are built into the brake pads and trigger a warning light on the dashboard when the pads have worn down to a certain level, indicating the need for replacement. Audible sensors, on the other hand, produce a squealing noise when the brake pads are worn out, serving as an audible signal for the driver to get them replaced. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring safe braking and preventing further damage to the braking system.</p></p>
