<p><strong>The UV Disinfection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ordinary Cathode Low Pressure Mercury UV Disinfection</li><li>High-intensity Ultraviolet Disinfection</li><li>Ozone UV Disinfection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV disinfection equipment market includes three types of technology. Ordinary Cathode Low Presure Mercury UV Disinfection utilizes low-pressure mercury lamps with quartz sleeves to emit UV light, effectively killing bacteria and viruses. High-intensity Ultraviolet Disinfection uses high-pressure mercury lamps, which produce more intense UV light for stronger disinfection. Ozone UV Disinfection combines UV light with ozone gas, forming hydroxyl radicals that destroy microorganisms. These technologies are used in various industries such as water treatment, healthcare, and food processing for effective disinfection purposes.</p></p>
