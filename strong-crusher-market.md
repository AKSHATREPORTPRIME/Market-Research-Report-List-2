V type blades are designed with a sharp edge angle for efficient cutting and shredding of materials. B type blades are designed with a blunt edge angle for powerful crushing and breaking of materials. A type blades are specifically designed for slicing and shearing materials. These different blade designs cater to varying crushing needs in the market, providing options for efficient and effective material processing.</p></p>
