<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multimode Fiber</li><li>Single-mode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber cable market is categorized into two types: multimode fiber and single-mode fiber. Multimode fiber is designed to carry multiple light modes simultaneously, allowing multiple signals to be transmitted at once. It is commonly used for shorter distances, such as LAN networks. On the other hand, single-mode fiber is designed to carry a single light mode, ensuring high transmission quality over long distances. It is widely used in applications like telecommunications and data centers where long-distance transmission is required. Both types cater to different needs in terms of distance and transmission capacity.</p></p>
