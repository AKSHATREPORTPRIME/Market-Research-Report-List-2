<p><p>Solar Photovoltaic (PV) Panels Market Types refer to the different types of solar panels available in the market. Mono-Si Modules are made from single-crystal silicon, which offers high efficiency and durability. Multi-Si Modules use multi-crystalline silicon, providing a cost-effective solution with slightly lower efficiency. CdTe Modules use cadmium telluride thin-film technology, offering lower costs but reduced efficiency. CIGS Modules use copper indium gallium selenide, providing flexibility and higher efficiency. A-Si Modules are made from amorphous silicon, offering flexibility and low light performance. Other types include emerging technologies like perovskite-based solar modules.</p></p>
