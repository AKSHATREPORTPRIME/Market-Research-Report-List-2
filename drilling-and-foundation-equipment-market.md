<p><p>Drilling and foundation equipment market encompasses different types of drilling techniques. Kelly drilling involves the use of a telescopic Kelly bar to transfer torque and crowd force to the drilling tool. Continuous Flight Auger drilling is a method where a continuous flight auger is used to excavate soil while injecting concrete into the hole. Double Rotary drilling utilizes two drilling systems to drill and simultaneously case the hole. Other drilling techniques include percussion drilling, sonic drilling, and reverse circulation drilling, each with their own unique methods and applications.</p></p>
