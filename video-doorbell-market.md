<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The video doorbell market can be classified into two main types: WiFi and DECT. WiFi video doorbells connect to a home's existing WiFi network, allowing users to access and control the doorbell's functions via smartphone or other devices. They offer convenient remote monitoring and two-way communication features. On the other hand, DECT (Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications) video doorbells use a dedicated frequency for communication and do not rely on WiFi. DECT video doorbells offer secure and reliable transmissions, making them a popular choice for users concerned about data privacy and interference issues.</p></p>
