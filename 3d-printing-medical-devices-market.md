<p><p>The 3D printing medical devices market can be categorized into two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable. Absorbable devices are made from materials that can be gradually broken down and absorbed by the body over time. They are commonly used for temporary implants and scaffolds. In contrast, non-absorbable devices are made from materials that do not degrade and remain in the body permanently. These devices are typically used for long-term applications like dental implants or joint replacements. Both types of devices offer unique advantages and cater to specific medical needs.</p></p>
