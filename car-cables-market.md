<p><ul><li>Copper Core</li><li>Aluminum Core</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car cables market encompasses three main types: copper core, aluminum core, and other materials. Copper core cables are widely used in automotive wiring due to their excellent electrical conductivity. They provide better signal transmission and lower resistance, ensuring optimal performance of various systems in vehicles. Aluminum core cables, on the other hand, offer cost advantages as they are less expensive and lighter than copper ones. However, aluminum cables have higher resistance and are therefore less efficient for high-power applications. The "other" market segment includes alternative materials such as fiber optic cables or composite materials with specific functionalities suited for particular automotive applications.</p></p>
