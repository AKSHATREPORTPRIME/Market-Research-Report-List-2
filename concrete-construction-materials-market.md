<p><p>The concrete construction materials market can be categorized into three types: ready-mix concrete, on-site mixed concrete, and others. Ready-mix concrete refers to the concrete that is produced in a centralized plant and delivered to the construction site, saving time and labor. On-site mixed concrete is produced at the construction site itself, allowing for more flexibility in adjusting the mix proportions. The "others" category may include alternative concrete materials like high-performance concrete or fiber-reinforced concrete, offering unique characteristics and applications in construction projects.</p></p>
