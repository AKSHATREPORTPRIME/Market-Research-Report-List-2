<p><p>An immunoprecipitation kit is a laboratory tool used to separate and purify specific proteins or nucleic acids from a complex mixture. Immunoprecipitation kits provide all the necessary reagents and instructions to perform this process efficiently.</p><p>The immunoprecipitation kit market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 8.80% during the forecast period.
