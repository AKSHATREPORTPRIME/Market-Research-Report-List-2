<p><p>Tilting disk check valves are available in three different market types: flanged end, threaded end, and welding end. </p><p>Flanged end check valves are designed with flanges on both ends, allowing them to be easily bolted onto piping systems, making installation and maintenance simpler.</p><p>Threaded end check valves have threaded connections on the ends, making them suitable for pipes with threaded connections. These valves can be easily screwed into place, offering convenience and versatility.</p><p>Welding end check valves have ends that can be welded directly onto the piping system, creating a strong and permanent connection. These valves are commonly used in applications where a secure and leak-proof joint is required.</p></p>
