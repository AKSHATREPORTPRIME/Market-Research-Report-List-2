Sinus bradycardia can be a normal physiological response to certain situations, such as during sleep or in highly trained athletes. However, it can also be a symptom of an underlying medical condition, including heart disease, hypothyroidism, certain medications, or excessive vagal tone.</p><p>The sinus bradycardia market is projected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
