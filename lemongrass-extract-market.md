<p><p>Lemongrass extract is available in two primary forms in the market: oil and hydrosol. Lemongrass oil is derived from the steam distillation process, where the essential oils of lemongrass are extracted and concentrated. It is commonly used in aromatherapy, perfumes, and skincare products due to its refreshing and rejuvenating scent. On the other hand, lemongrass hydrosol is created through the steam distillation of the entire plant, retaining water-soluble components. It is commonly used in skincare and haircare products as a mild and hydrating ingredient. Both forms offer distinct benefits and are sought after in various industries.</p></p>
