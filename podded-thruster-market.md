<p><p>Podded thrusters are used in marine vessels for propulsion and maneuvering. The market for podded thrusters can be categorized into two types: built-in motor and external motor. In the built-in motor market, the motor is enclosed within the pod and directly drives the propeller. This design provides compactness and efficiency. On the other hand, the external motor market involves a separate motor connected to the thruster via a shaft. This design allows flexibility in terms of motor size and location, which may be advantageous for certain vessel designs.</p></p>
