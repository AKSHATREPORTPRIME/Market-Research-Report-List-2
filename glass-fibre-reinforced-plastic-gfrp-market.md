The GFRP market can be categorized into two types: Thermosetting Plastic Products and Thermoplastic Plastic Products. Thermosetting plastic products are made by curing through a heat-induced chemical reaction, resulting in a rigid and durable material. On the other hand, thermoplastic plastic products can be repeatedly melted and solidified without undergoing a chemical change, providing flexibility and ease of molding. These market types cater to various industries, including automotive, construction, aerospace, and electrical sectors.</p></p>
