<p><p>The pharmaceutical foil market consists of two main types: cold forming aluminum foil and tropical aluminum foil. Cold forming aluminum foil is primarily used for packaging pharmaceutical products that require high barrier properties to protect them from moisture, oxygen, and light. It is characterized by its malleability and ability to form into blister packs without losing its barrier properties. On the other hand, tropical aluminum foil is designed for tropical climates, providing excellent resistance to high humidity and temperature conditions. It ensures the stability and effectiveness of pharmaceutical products in such environments.</p></p>
