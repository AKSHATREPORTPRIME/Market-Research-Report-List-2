<p><ul><li>Low Current Load</li><li>Standard Load</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Miniature switches are categorized into two main types based on their load capacity: Low Current Load and Standard Load. Low Current Load switches are designed to handle small electrical currents, typically less than 5A. These switches are commonly used in electronic devices that require low-power switching, such as smartphones, digital cameras, and remote controls. On the other hand, Standard Load switches are capable of carrying higher currents, typically ranging from 5A to 25A. They are more suitable for industrial equipment, appliances, and machinery that require higher power switching capabilities.</p></p>
