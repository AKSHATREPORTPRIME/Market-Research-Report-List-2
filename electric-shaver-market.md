<p><p>The electric shaver market is divided into two main types: foil and rotary shavers. Foil shavers use a thin layer of vibrating metal (the foil) with small holes to capture and cut the hair. They provide a close and precise shave, making them ideal for those with sensitive skin. On the other hand, rotary shavers have multiple round heads that move independently, adapting to facial contours. They are more suitable for longer hair and are generally considered more comfortable and efficient for shaving larger areas.</p></p>
