<p><p>The Small-Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (SUAV) market can be categorized into four types. The first type is the Light Fixed-Wing SUAV, which includes compact and agile drones designed for surveillance and reconnaissance missions. The second type is the Heavy Fixed-Wing SUAV, comprising larger and more robust drones used for long-range missions like cargo delivery. The third type is the Multi-Rotor VTOL SUAV, featuring drones capable of vertical take-off and landing. These drones are commonly used for aerial photography and inspections. Finally, the Nano SUAV market encompasses tiny and lightweight drones used for indoor and close-quarter operations, such as monitoring inside buildings.</p></p>
