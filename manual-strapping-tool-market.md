<p><p>The manual strapping tool market includes two main types: mechanical strapping tools and hand pneumatic strapping tools. Mechanical strapping tools are operated manually and require physical force to tighten and seal the straps. They are simple to operate, cost-effective, and suitable for light to medium applications. On the other hand, hand pneumatic strapping tools use compressed air to tension and seal the straps, offering higher tension force and efficiency. These tools are ideal for heavy-duty applications and can handle various types of strapping material.</p></p>
