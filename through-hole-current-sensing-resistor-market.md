<p><p>The through-hole current sensing resistor market comprises three main types: metal alloy, metal foil, and film. Metal alloy resistors are made from a combination of metals and offer high precision and stability. Metal foil resistors consist of a thin metal foil layer on a ceramic substrate and provide excellent long-term stability and low temperature coefficient. Film resistors are made from a metal film deposited on a non-conductive substrate and offer good precision and stability. These different types cater to various applications and requirements within the current sensing resistor market.</p></p>
