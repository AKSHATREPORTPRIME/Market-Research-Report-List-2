<p><p>Fiber Reinforced Plastics (FRP) are composite materials made of a polymer matrix reinforced with fibers such as glass, carbon, or aramid fibers. The increasing focus on lightweight and fuel-efficient vehicles, along with stringent regulatory standards for emissions, is driving the demand for FRP in the automotive industry. Moreover, the growing need for sustainable and energy-efficient building materials is expected to boost the adoption of FRP in the construction sector. Additionally, the increasing usage of FRP in wind turbine blades, oil & gas pipes, and water treatment plants is further fueling the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, technological advancements in manufacturing processes and the development of bio-based FRP materials are expected to create new opportunities in the market. The use of automation and robotics in FRP manufacturing processes is improving efficiency and reducing costs, which is likely to contribute to the market's growth.</p><p>However, there are certain challenges that the FRP market may face.
