<p><p>Polyphenylene Sulfide (PPS) fibers are categorized into two types: PPS filaments and PPS staple fibers. PPS filaments are continuous strands of PPS fiber with a uniform and smooth texture, making them suitable for applications such as textiles, filters, and cables. On the other hand, PPS staple fibers are shorter lengths of PPS fiber, allowing for better blending with other materials in applications such as automotive parts, electrical components, and insulation. Both PPS filaments and PPS staple fibers are widely used in various industries due to their high thermal and chemical resistance, excellent dimensional stability, and flame retardant properties.</p></p>
