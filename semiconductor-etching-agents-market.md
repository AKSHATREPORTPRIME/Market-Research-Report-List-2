<p><ul><li>Wet Etching Agent</li><li>Dry Etching Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor etching agents are used in the manufacturing process of semiconductors to selectively remove materials from the surface. Wet etching agents refer to the use of liquid-based chemicals to etch the semiconductor surface. They are economical but may result in less precise etching. On the other hand, dry etching agents involve the use of gases or plasma to remove materials. This method offers higher precision and control but can be more expensive. Both wet and dry etching agents are crucial in semiconductor fabrication, with their choice depending on specific requirements and cost considerations.</p></p>
