<p><p>The DC Link Capacitors market can be categorized into three types: fixed capacitors, variable capacitors, and trimmer capacitors. </p><p>Fixed capacitors are designed to have a fixed value of capacitance, which means the capacitance remains constant under normal operating conditions. </p><p>Variable capacitors, on the other hand, have the ability to adjust their capacitance value. This provides flexibility in different applications where variable capacitance is required. </p><p>Trimmer capacitors are a subtype of variable capacitors that provide precise adjustment of capacitance. They are generally used in circuits that require fine-tuning or calibration.</p></p>
