<p><p>PU resins are essential components of faux leather products and are used in two main market types: dry-process synthetic leather and wet-process synthetic leather. In the dry-process method, PU resins are mixed with other materials and then applied onto fabric or paper substrates. Through a drying or curing process, a layer of faux leather is formed. On the other hand, wet-process synthetic leather involves applying the PU resins directly onto a release paper and transferring it onto a fabric substrate. Both methods result in the creation of high-quality and sustainable faux leather products.</p></p>
