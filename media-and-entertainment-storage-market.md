<p><ul><li>Network Attached Storage</li><li>Storage Area Network</li><li>Direct Attached Storage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Media and Entertainment Storage market primarily consists of three types: Network Attached Storage (NAS), Storage Area Network (SAN), and Direct Attached Storage (DAS). NAS involves a dedicated device connected to a network that allows multiple users to access storage. SAN utilizes a separate network to provide storage to multiple servers/devices, offering scalability and centralized management. DAS simply connects storage directly to a single device, often via USB or Thunderbolt. Each of these storage types caters to different needs and requirements in the media and entertainment industry.</p></p>
