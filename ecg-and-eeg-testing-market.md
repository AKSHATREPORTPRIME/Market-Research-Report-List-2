<p><p>ECG and EEG testing are two commonly used medical diagnostic techniques that measure the electrical activity of the heart and brain, respectively. ECG (electrocardiography) records the electrical impulses generated by the heart and helps in diagnosing various heart conditions such as arrhythmias, cardiac hypertrophy, and myocardial infarction. EEG (electroencephalography) records the electrical activity of the brain and is used in the evaluation of epilepsy, sleep disorders, and brain tumors.</p><p>The global ECG and EEG testing market has witnessed significant growth in recent years due to the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular and neurological disorders, along with growing awareness about early disease diagnosis. The market is driven by technological advancements in ECG and EEG devices, such as wireless and portable systems, which enhance patient comfort and ease of use. Additionally, the rising geriatric population, who are more prone to cardiovascular and neurological diseases, is fueling market growth.</p><p>The market for ECG and EEG testing is also witnessing the incorporation of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) technologies for improved data analysis and interpretation.
