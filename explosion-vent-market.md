<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The explosion vent market comprises various types of vents used for safety purposes. Self-destructive vents are designed to rupture once a predetermined pressure is reached and cannot be resealed. Non-self-re-closing vents are similar but can be manually closed after use. Re-usable vents, as the name suggests, can be reset and reused multiple times. Lastly, self-re-closing vents provide automatic resealing after they have relieved pressure, ensuring continued protection. These different types cater to diverse safety needs in industries where explosions may occur.</p></p>
