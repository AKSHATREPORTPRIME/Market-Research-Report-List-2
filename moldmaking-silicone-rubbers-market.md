<p><p>Moldmaking silicone rubbers are widely used in various industries for creating molds for different products. There are two types available in the market, namely solid silicone rubber and liquid silicone rubber. Solid silicone rubber is a firm and non-flowing material that requires curing to form the mold. On the other hand, liquid silicone rubber is a pourable and self-leveling substance that cures at room temperature and is ideal for intricate mold designs. Both types have their own advantages and suitability depending on the specific molding requirements.</p></p>
