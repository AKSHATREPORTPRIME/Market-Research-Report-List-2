<p><p>Smoke evacuation systems are devices designed to remove surgical smoke, which is created during various types of medical procedures. The market types of smoke evacuation systems include electrosurgical units and laparoscopic surgery market. Electrosurgical units use high-frequency electric currents to cut, coagulate, and vaporize tissue, creating smoke that needs to be evacuated. Laparoscopic surgery involves accessing the abdominal cavity through small incisions, and the smoke produced during this procedure also needs to be removed. Both these market types play a crucial role in maintaining a clear surgical field and protecting surgical staff from potential health hazards associated with surgical smoke.</p></p>
