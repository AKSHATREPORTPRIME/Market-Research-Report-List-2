<p><p>The 3D laser marking head market is broadly categorized into two types: 2 axes and 3 axes. On the other hand, the 3 axes marking heads enable laser marking in X, Y, and Z dimensions, making them ideal for curved or irregular surfaces. These heads offer more flexibility and accuracy in marking complex shapes and are commonly used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics for product identification and traceability purposes.</p></p>
