<p><p>Mesh Wi-Fi routers are available in different market types namely Bi-Band, Tri-Band, and Quad-Band. Bi-Band routers use two frequency bands, typically 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz, to connect devices and offer basic internet coverage. Tri-Band routers have one additional 5 GHz band, reducing congestion and improving performance in densely connected environments. Quad-Band routers have an extra 60 GHz band, enabling even faster speeds and increased capacity for multiple devices. The market types represent varying levels of capabilities and are designed to cater to different user needs for enhanced Wi-Fi connectivity.</p></p>
