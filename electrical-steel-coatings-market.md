<p><p>Electrical steel coatings are used to protect the steel from corrosion and improve its electrical properties. These coatings are categorized into different types based on their composition and performance. </p><p>C2 coatings are thin, organic coatings that provide minimal protection but enable good adhesion. C3 coatings offer moderate corrosion protection with a slightly thicker organic coating. C4 coatings provide good corrosion resistance with a thick film of organic coating. C5 coatings have an additional barrier layer, offering higher protection against aggressive environments. C6 coatings are highly specialized and provide superior corrosion resistance, suitable for extreme conditions.</p></p>
