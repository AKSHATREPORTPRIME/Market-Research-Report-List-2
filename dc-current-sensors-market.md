<p><p>DC current sensors are devices used to measure the flow of direct current in electrical circuits. Closed-loop current sensors utilize a feedback mechanism to continuously adjust the output based on the input, providing accurate measurements at high currents. In contrast, open-loop current sensors provide a proportional output without using feedback, making them suitable for low-current applications. Both types play a crucial role in various industries such as automotive, industrial automation, and energy management, by ensuring efficient power usage and enhancing system performance.</p></p>
