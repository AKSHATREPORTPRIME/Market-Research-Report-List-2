<p><p>The Light Ionization Detection Sensor market can be divided into two main types: Fixed Light Ionization Detection Sensor and Portable Light Ionization Detection Sensor. </p><p>Fixed Light Ionization Detection Sensors are permanently installed in a particular area to continuously monitor the presence of ionizing radiation. They are commonly used in nuclear power plants, medical facilities, and laboratories where radiation levels need to be constantly monitored for safety purposes. </p><p>On the other hand, Portable Light Ionization Detection Sensors are designed to be compact and handheld, allowing users to easily carry and use them in various locations. They are commonly used by first responders, emergency personnel, and hazmat teams to quickly assess radiation levels at different sites.</p></p>
